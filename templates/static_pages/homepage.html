PsychoModels

The model repository for psychological science

We aim to create a database that encourages sharing, reusing, and extending computational implementations of formal models in psychology. By creating a platform designed for productive exchange within modeling groups and between modelers and empirical researchers, modeling practices among researchers will flourish and expand.
